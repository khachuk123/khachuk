# all about my lifting (khachuk)
my lifting journey started with simple goals to get stronger and stay consistent. it's been going better than imagined, along the way i've gained not just strength, but a deep understanding of training, nutrition, recovery, and discipline. here is a short guidance.
# [1/5] Programing advice
Depend on your schedule (recommend 3-6 days a week)

High Frequency (6-7 days a week)
Lower volume intensity (3-5/10 in difficulty, or following a split, i.e. push-pull-legs-rest)

low Frequency (2-3 days aweek)
Higher volume or intensity (7-8/10 in difficulty,full body workouts)
